About

Turkey Vulture is Dustin Ray. An artist, musician, and writer from Elgin, Oklahoma — third generation of artists in his family. He lives and works in Lawton with his wife, three children, two dogs, and a cat.

The work sits at the edge of things: nature and the human, folklore and flatland, the candlelit and the desolate. Watercolor and ink mostly, with oil and charcoal when the subject calls for it. Landscapes that feel inhabited by something. Figures that are almost there.

Shaped by the isolation and silence particular to the southern plains — and by what the 2020s have done to the distance between people and the natural world.

Third generation. Lawton, Oklahoma. Still at it.
